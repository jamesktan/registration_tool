East Bay Cluster Retreat Signup Form


The East Bay Cluster Retreat is a gathering intended to promote the unity of the cluster as well as its devotional character. Through songs, stories, study, activities, and the drafting of plans, we hope that every member in our cluster will develop deep bonds of friendship with one another and identify personal contributions that can be made to strengthen the community building efforts in the East Bay.

Details:

  • Venue: Bosch Baha'i School
  • Days: April 27 (evening) to April 29
  • Check In: 8PM April 27
  • Check Out: 2PM April 29
  • Meals Provided

The last day to register is 4/13/18.
